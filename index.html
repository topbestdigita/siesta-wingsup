About The Prestige Group

Prestige Group is a prominent real estate developer in India with 32 years of rich experience. It has established a strong industry presence across prominent cities like Cochin, Bangalore, Chennai, Goa, Mangalore and Hyderabad by developing award-winning projects. The builder has diverse portfolio covering retail, residential, commercial, leisure and hospitality sectors. An ISO 9001:2000 certified company, Prestige Group is the only real estate developer in Bangalore to receive the FIABCI Award for its software and residential facilities. It was also honoured with the Crisil DA1 Developer Rating for delivering quality constructions on a timely basis. Prestige Group also bagged the “Admired Brand of Asia” in the category of Real Estate at the Asian Brand Conclave. Founded by Razack Sattar, Prestige Group builds homes that are of globally accepted standards with the best and wide-ranging choices for families looking for a quality lifestyle. The developer has a customer focused approach with a passion for new ideas and explores new possibilities in creating projects of world-class quality, at a price that suits your budget. It strives towards setting new benchmarks for quality as well as continuously enhance the customer experiences, through constant innovation and transparency
